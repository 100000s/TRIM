# TRIM
TRIM (Tax Reduction Incentive Mechanism) is an ERC20-compatible token blockchain that rewards politicians for reducing tax burdens and budget deficits, encouraging them to put their skin in the game. Instead of mining blocks with difficult calculations (PoW) or requiring significant token holdings (PoS), TRIM mints tokens entirely in accordence with tax reductions and sends them to the legislators responsible as a record of achievement. The legislators may claim their tokens anonymously, if they wish, and trade or spend them anonymously. All mining fees are determined competitively and split between the consensus infrastructure (Raspberry Pis and smartphone operators). This is separated from TRIMs economic engine (the fiscal oracle system). The escrowed and oraclized smart contract rewards federal, state, county, and municipal officeholders, and allows participation from foreign jurisdictions.
